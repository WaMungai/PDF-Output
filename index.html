5. THIS PERMIT IS NOT APPLICABLE TO FOREST PRODUCE PURCHASED FROM A LICENSED SAW MILLER AND ACCOMPANIED BY AN OFFICIAL DELIVERY NOTE.

8. TRANSPORT OF FOREST PRODUCE MUST BE DONE DURING DAY-TIME HOURS (FROM 6:00 AM TO 6:00 PM).

9. THE CHARGE FOR MOVEMENT OF THE FOREST PRODUCE WILL BE DETERMINED AS PER THE FOREST SERVICE GENERAL ORDER (FSGO).
